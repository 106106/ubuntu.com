Keep your GCP Ubuntu 16.04 LTS systems secure

If you would like to keep using Ubuntu 16.04 LTS, Canonical is delighted to offer three-additional years of Extended Security Maintenance (ESM) available with the Ubuntu Pro 16.04 LTS premium image or through an Ubuntu Advantage subscription.

The best option depends on Ubuntu 16.04 LTS compute spend. Ubuntu Pro 16.04 is priced at 3.0-4.5% of of your average compute cost of your existing 16.04 LTS instances, or alternatively you can get an Ubuntu Advantage subscription starting at $75 per VM per year. You can also consider migrating to Ubuntu 18.04 LTS or Ubuntu 20.04 LTS, which are both under standard maintenance until 2023 and 2025 respectively.

If you are looking to upgrade your Ubuntu 16.04 LTS public cloud instance, it is recommended to launch new, Ubuntu Pro images. Ubuntu Pro for GCP is a premium image with security and compliance features built in, and optimised for the public cloud.

Ubuntu Pro includes security coverage for the entire collection of software packages shipped with Ubuntu, tracking high and critical CVEs. Ideal for companies that have embraced open source for their new products.
