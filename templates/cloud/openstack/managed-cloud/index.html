Get a private BootStack managed cloud in three easy steps:

  1. We build your cloud

    Together, we design your cloud to the required size and specifications — on your site, in your datacentre or hosted with one of our partners.

  2. We operate your cloud

    Our OpenStack experts take full responsibility for the management, monitoring and 24x7 availability of your new cloud.

  3. We transfer control

    As and when you decide to take control of your cloud back in house, we will train your team on its operations to ensure a smooth handover.

BootStack can be customised to your requirements:

Hosted BootStack

We have chosen QTS and eShelter as a hosted option for BootStack worldwide, providing the highest performing dedicated cloud infrastructure available.

Their customisation options enable OpenStack to be used for provisioning bare metal as well as virtual machines from data centres around the world.

Download QTS brochure ›

BootStack Direct

Whereas a Hosted BootStack leaves the operations to us, BootStack Direct guarantees the operation of the cloud goes directly to you, including training and support for your staff.

We would get your OpenStack cloud up and running in days, preparing your team to become OpenStack operations experts.

BootStack for Kubernetes

Whether you want to orchestrate your container cluster on top of your OpenStack cloud or other substrate we’ll build and operate it for you using the Canonical Distribution of Kubernetes. Focus on your applications not infrastructure.
