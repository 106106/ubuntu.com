• Prepare the SD Card

    Warning
    Following these steps will erase any existing content on the removable drive!

    • Insert the microSD card into your computer
    • Run the imager and click 'CHOOSE OS' {{ image( url="https://assets.ubuntu.com/v1/1148ca79-1_.png", alt="CHOOSE OS", width="750", height="505", hi_def=True, loading="lazy" ) | safe}}
    • Next, choose 'Use Custom'. Select the image you downloaded.
    • Select your SDcard and click 'WRITE'. {{ image( url="https://assets.ubuntu.com/v1/8f1a170c-raspberry-pi-imager-step2.png", alt="Custom", width="750", height="494", hi_def=True, loading="lazy", ) | safe}}

    While it is preparing the disk you can go on to the next step and create your SSH keys for secure access to the appliance.
