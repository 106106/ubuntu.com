Install Ubuntu on Xilinx

Run Ubuntu on your Xilinx Zynq UltraScale+ MPSoC-based evaluation boards and Kria production SOMs.

Pick the OS image to match your hardware, flash it onto SD/microSD card, load it onto your board and away you go.
