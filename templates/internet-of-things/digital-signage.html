Signage players need the power of Ubuntu

  • Small footprint, serious power: reducing your hardware costs while still running 4k video
  • Remote maintenance: no need for on-site personnel
  • Security: closes the door to screen takeovers and other attacks
  • Stability: designed for 24/7 availability
  • Software development: existing libraries and codebase, flexibility in development, all the way down to the device driver
  • Atomic upgradability: low bandwidth, prompt delivery of new features
  • Low cost: open source digital signage
  • Application isolation enables security, robustness and simpler maintenance
  • Faster innovation: wide peripheral support, Wi-Fi, 3G/4G, NFC, Bluetooth, software-defined radio, deep learning, audio and camera support (including RealSense)

Simplified software management

Ubuntu Core gives your screens the uptime and robustness you need. Uniquely stable and secure, it is based on the same operating system that runs the majority of the world's clouds.

Ubuntu Core adds the ability to upgrade the entire software stack remotely and securely, from the kernel all the way up to the apps. Canary rollout and rollback capability gives you peace of mind during upgrades: if anything goes wrong, the device automatically reverts to the last working release. With update management facilities to manage roll-out as you wish, Ubuntu Core is the ultimate digital signage OS.
